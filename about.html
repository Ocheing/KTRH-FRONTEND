Welcome to KTRH

Kisii Teaching & Referral Hospital (KTRH) is a premier healthcare institution dedicated to providing comprehensive medical services, medical education, and research in the Western Kenya region.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services through innovative medical practices, excellence in training, and cutting-edge research while maintaining compassion and respect for all.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare institution in the region, recognized for excellence in patient care, medical education, research, and community service.

Our Values

  • Compassion: Care with empathy and kindness
  • Excellence: Strive for the highest standards
  • Integrity: Uphold ethical practices
  • Teamwork: Collaborate for better outcomes
  • Innovation: Embrace new technologies and methods

Our History

Journey of growth and excellence in healthcare

2005

Establishment

Kisii Teaching & Referral Hospital was established as a county referral facility to serve the growing healthcare needs of the region.

2010

Teaching Hospital Designation

Recognized as a teaching hospital, partnering with medical schools to train the next generation of healthcare professionals.

2015

Major Expansion

Completed major infrastructure expansion, adding specialized units including ICU, NICU, and modern operating theaters.

2018

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive Hospital Information System (HIS) and electronic medical records for improved patient care.

2022

Modern Facilities

Opened new diagnostic center with MRI, CT Scan, and advanced laboratory facilities.

2024

Ongoing Growth

Continuing to expand services and facilities to meet the evolving healthcare needs of our community.
